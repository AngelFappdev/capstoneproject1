Angel's Angelic Petals serves to deliver you high-quality flowers, floral arrangements, and bouquets for your convenience! We sell roses, white, red, yellow, and pink. We create bouquets for weddings and arrangements for dates or graduations. We are locally sourced and work with a dedicated group of gardeners. We have a storefront located at 111 flowers st. Garden City, NY 11501. We have in-person pickings, bouquet creations, and arrangements as well.

Our mission is to allow you to express your affection for you're loved one or even a potential loved one.We want to enable you to show how much you care about your loved ones, teachers, partners, and parents. We want your loved ones to feel appreciated with our beautiful, locally grown, arrangements. We created our site in 2021 because we felt that this industry needed more love and care. When a customer purchases an arrangement from us we give you a complimentary gift that shows you how to increase the longevity of your flowers. Thank you!
